About Us

Autonomous Drone Racing (ADR) is a robotics team within the University of Toronto Aerial Team (UTAT). Formed by passionate roboticists, drone enthusiasts, graduate/undergraduate engineering students with a shared ambition: to build the fastest autonomous racing drone in the world capable of reliability outflying human pilots.

We compete at international autonomous drone racing competitions including the upcoming one hosted by A2RL, the Abu Dhabi Autonomous Racing League, in April 2025, which requires an FPV drone equipped with camera and onboard computer to autonomously pass through a sequence of gates in clustered environments with shortest time possible.
